About my work

My work changes, in some aspects, between the summer and winter seasons. During the winter season the work is focused on the development of the management system, and therefore, on the tests to be done on the new features or changes made to the management system. Basically, these are end-to-end tests, to verify the functioning of the various flows of operations, and API tests (as part of ¬´integration testing¬ª), to verify the correct data flow. During the summer season, my work is focused, partially, on providing technical assistance to managers who are experiencing problems, collecting and analyzing data to improve the product.
