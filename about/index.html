About me

Born in Cattolica (RN) on 12/13/1994, in 2013 I graduated from the then Technical Institute for Surveyors: Odone Belluzzi of Rimini (today State Technical Technological Institute ). I studied at the University of Cesena, Alma Mater Studiorum, in the course of Engineering and Computer Science . I currently work as a CTO and full-stack web developer for an innovative startup that deals with beach management and online booking of establishments: Beacharound S.r.l.

About my work

As mentioned above, i currently work for an innovative startup that sells a management system for bath establishments and online bookings: Beacharound S.r.l. During my winter working days I take care of testing new management software functions, reporting bugs to developers (sometimes developing some functions myself) and providing technical assistance to business customers. During the summer, the company concentrates its resources on the management of online bookings (business side and customer side). In that case, my work also aligns with these needs and I spend my time providing technical assistance by reporting bugs to the developers.
